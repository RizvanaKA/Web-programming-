Pakistan

Pakistan is a country in Asia and its neighbour countries are India, China, and Afghanistan. In Pakistan, there are so many different places, like mountains and deserts. 220 million people live in Pakistan. Pakistan became its own country in 1947 after being ruled by England. But nowadays Pakistan is facing so many problems like enough money. But Pakistan is growing in some things like farming. People of Pakistan speak different languages like Urdu and Hindi and Pakistanis have different ways of living. They celebrate so many festivals, enjoy music, and eat delicious food. Pakistan is a nice place to visit.

Firstly, Pakistan has a wonderful history. Pakistan gained independence from British rule in 1947. After Independence, it becomes a fairly young country. The country’s name ‘Pakistan’ means ‘Land of the Pure’ in Urdu. Urdu is one of its official languages. Pakistan is a place of various cultures and traditions. People from different backgrounds, such as Punjabi, Sindhi, Balochi, and Pashtun, live in Pakistan together. Each culture has its own unique music, dance, clothing, and delicious food which make Pakistan a vibrant and colourful nation. Pakistan has lots of amazing places to visit. In the north, there are huge mountains called the Himalayas, and in the south, there are amazing beaches by the Arabian Sea. One special place is Mohenjo-Daro which is an old city that is a part of history. It is important that it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. And in Lahore, there are many busy streets with very old buildings of Mughal architecture. There are many amazing yummy street foods.
